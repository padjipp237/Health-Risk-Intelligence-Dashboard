# 🧠 Health & Lifestyle Risk Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **health analytics dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze the relationship between lifestyle behaviors and chronic disease risk.

The solution goes beyond traditional dashboards by integrating a **risk scoring system** and **AI-style recommendations** to simulate real-world healthcare decision-making tools.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify high-risk individuals based on lifestyle factors
* Analyze the impact of behavior (smoking, exercise, diet) on health outcomes
* Provide actionable health recommendations
* Support preventive healthcare strategies

---

## 📊 Dataset

* 7,500 synthetic individuals
* Features include:

  * Demographics (Age, Gender)
  * Health metrics (BMI, Sleep, Stress)
  * Lifestyle habits (Smoking, Exercise, Diet, Alcohol)
  * Chronic disease indicator

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

### 🔹 Risk Scoring System

A custom-built scoring model combining:

* Smoking status
* Exercise frequency
* Diet quality
* Sleep duration
* Stress level
* BMI

### 🔹 Risk Segmentation

* Low Risk
* Moderate Risk
* High Risk

### 🔹 AI-Style Recommendations

Dynamic recommendations generated based on individual risk profiles.

### 🟡 Lifestyle Impact Analysis

### 🔴 Risk Intelligence

* Risk segmentation
* High-risk population analysis
* Lifestyle risk heatmap

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Smoking and lack of exercise are primary drivers of chronic disease
* Poor diet strongly correlates with higher BMI and risk levels
* High stress combined with low sleep significantly increases health risk
